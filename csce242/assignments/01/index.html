Solid Snake and Big Boss are different characters

People tend to get the characters Big Boss and Solid snake from the Metal Gear franchise mixed. Even journalists mixup the two.

Solid Snake

Solid Snake is a video game character from the Metal Gear franchise. He is the son/clone of Big Boss. The games he is the protagonist of take place between the years of 1995-2014. While looking similar to and having similar skills as Big Boss they are not the same character.

Big Boss

Big Boss is a video game character from the Metal Gear franchise. He is the father/DNA donor of Solid Snake. The games he is the protagonist of take place between the years 1964-1984. Big Boss also has body double made with plastic surgery and brainwashing that is named Venom Snake that he looks nearly identical to.

List of things to differentiate the characters with

  • Big Boss when wearing an eye patch wears it on his right eye where as when Solid Snake wears his solid he wears in on his left eye.
  • Big Boss smokes cigars where as Solid Snake smokes lucky strike cigarettes.
  • Big Boss wears modified US Army BDU where as Solid Snake wears a high tech sneaking suit
